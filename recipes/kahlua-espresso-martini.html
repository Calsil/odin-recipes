Kahlua Espresso martini

Description

Made with three key ingredients: Kahlua coffee liquer, Absolut Vodka and Lavazza Espresso. An old classic. Dangerously good

Ingredients

  • 25ml Kahlua
  • 25ml Absolut Vodka
  • 25ml Lavazza Espresso
  • Ice cubes
  • 3 Coffee Beans

Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients and ice into a shaker
  2. Shake for 15-20 seconds, for that velvety foam
  3. Strain into a martini or cocktail glass
  4. Garnish with 3 coffee beans, for good luck
