3 Ingredient Banana Pancakes

Description

A light, cheap, and easy to make dish made from Monkey's favourite fruits! (Technically berries)

Ingredients

  • 3 Bananas
  • 3 Eggs
  • 6 Tbsp Self-Raising Flour
  • Any other toppings you want

Steps

  1. Mash Up the bananas in a bowl and add in the eggs and flour. Whisk until combined
  2. Heat a large son-stick pan over medium heat, brush over some vegetable oil, and add a ladle of the pancake batter
  3. Cook on low/medium heat for 3 mins on either side
  4. Serve with your favourite toppings
